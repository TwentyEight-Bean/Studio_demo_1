# SEO Post Rollout Plan

## Muc tieu
- Tang impression va click tu nhom tu khoa local intent cao.
- Xay content cluster giup trang dich vu va portfolio tang topical authority.
- Tao luong chuyen doi tu bai viet sang trang lien hep.

## Kien truc bai viet
- `Pillar service`: bai tong hop cho tung dich vu chinh.
- `Location pages`: bai theo khu vuc thanh pho co nhu cau cao.
- `Guide posts`: bai huong dan tra loi truy van thong tin.
- `Case-study`: bai phan tich du an that, lien ket ve dich vu.

## Khung SEO on-page cho moi post
- URL dang slug khong dau.
- Title 55-60 ky tu, keyword chinh dat gan dau.
- Meta description 140-155 ky tu, co CTA nhe.
- Cau truc heading ro: H1 duy nhat, H2/H3 theo intent.
- Them FAQ block cuoi bai neu co truy van hoi dap.
- Internal links:
  - 2 link ve bai lien quan
  - 1 link ve trang dich vu
  - 1 link ve trang lien he

## Lich xuat ban 4 tuan
- Tuan 1:
  - 3 bai pillar (service + location chinh)
  - 2 bai guide long-tail
- Tuan 2:
  - 3 bai location mo rong
  - 2 bai case-study portfolio
- Tuan 3:
  - 4 bai guide theo cau hoi GSC
  - cap nhat internal links cho bai tuan 1-2
- Tuan 4:
  - rewrite title/meta cho bai CTR thap
  - mo rong FAQ schema cho bai co impression cao

## Do luong va toi uu
- Theo doi GSC:
  - impressions
  - clicks
  - CTR
  - average position
- Theo doi analytics:
  - `contact_click`
  - `form_submit`
  - `portfolio_view`
- Chu ky toi uu: moi 7 ngay cap nhat snippet va internal links cho top 20 URL co impression cao.
